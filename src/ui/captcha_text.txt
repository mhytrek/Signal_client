Captcha challenge is required to continue using Signal.
If you don't submit the challenge you will be able to
send messages only to people who have messaged you
during the current session.

Press ESC to return to main screen.

1. Open https://signalcaptchas.org/challenge/generate
   - Press 'y' to copy URL to clipboard
2. Complete the captcha challenge.
3. Upon completion close the popup window when one shows up.
4. Right click the "Open Signal"
   button that appears under the captcha box and
   copy the link.
5. Paste the link in the input field below (press 'p' to
   paste from clipboard) and press ENTER.
